About

The Luca Yeager Band is about trying to decide between hearing tonight or having hearing in 20 years. At a show, you'll see frontman Luca Yeager taking his earplugs in and out struggling with this decision. The band is "bringing energy back to the modern stage" (NYSMusic) with inspirations from late '60s and early '70s psychedelic rock and the underlying blues and folk that began it all.
