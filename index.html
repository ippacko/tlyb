About

The Luca Yeager Band is hitting the stage in New York City. Led by guitarist and songwriter Luca Yeager, the group delivers a raw, immersive sound rooted in psychedelic rock and electric blues.

A graduate of NYU’s songwriting program, Luca blends technical craft with emotional depth, shaping music that’s built to draw you in. The band’s lineup features fellow NYU music grads, each bringing their own deep pockets of influence. Together, they’ve crafted a live experience that’s both hypnotic and high‑voltage.

Their debut album, The Free Trial, is a soulful tribute to the sounds that shaped them — layered with fuzzed‑out guitars, shifting rhythms, and lyrical fire. Onstage, they’re tight, expressive, and unpredictable. This is a band built for live performance, and they’re out to make every show an experience.
